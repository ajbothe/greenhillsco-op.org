Tuition

Three-year-olds (2-day): $115 per month ($1035 per year)

Four-year-olds (3-day): $140 per month ($1260 per year)

Pre-K (4 Day) Four-Five year-olds: $175 per month ($1575 per year)

Pre-K (5 day) Four-Five-year-olds: $220 per month ($1980 per year)



Tuition is paid online with Smart Tuition. Visit SmartTuition.com to log in to your account and manage your tuition payments. The Greenhills Co-op code is 12111.

Monthly tuition is due on the first class day of each month September - May. If tuition is paid in full by August 15, the Smart Tuition enrollment fee of $50 will be waived. Families with two children enrolled during the current school year will receive a 5% tuition discount on the youngest child’s tuition.
