Tuition

Three-year-olds: $110 per month ($990 per year)

Four-year-olds: $135 per month ($1,215 per year)

Five-year-olds: $165 per month ($1,485 per year)



Tuition is paid online with Smart Tuition. Visit SmartTuition.com to log in to your account and manage your tuition payments. The Greenhills Co-op code is 12111.

Monthly tuition is due on the first class day of each month September - May. If tuition is paid in full by August 15, the Smart Tuition enrollment fee of $45 will be waived. Families with two children enrolled during the current school year will receive a 5% tuition discount on the youngest child’s tuition.
